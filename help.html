Kirti's Fashion World Help System

Getting Started

Welcome to Kirti's Fashion World. This page is designed to help you navigate the system and get the most out of your experience.

Using the Search Bar

The search bar is located at the top of every page. You can use it to search for specific makeup products by name or category. Simply type in your search query and hit enter.

Browsing Products

You can browse our selection of makeup products by category. Simply click on the category you're interested in, and you'll be taken to a page displaying all products in that category. From there, you can click on individual products to view more information about them.

Placing an Order

To place an order, simply add the desired products to your cart and proceed to checkout. You'll be prompted to enter your shipping and payment information, and then your order will be processed.
